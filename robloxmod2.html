Exchange everything through Robux

Robux, often abbreviated as “R$” is the official virtual currency of the Roblox platform. It plays an important role in buying, selling, and creating items in the game and the platform. Accordingly, players can use Robux to buy decorations for their characters. Examples include exclusive game access, purchasing special in-game artifacts, or expanding functionality in Roblox Studio.

There are several ways to earn Robux:

  1. Buy Robux directly through Roblox.
  2. Join Roblox Premium, Roblox’s monthly subscription service, which gives users a specific monthly amount of Robux.
  3. Sell items, like clothes or accessories, on the Roblox Marketplace.
  4. Create and sell games or artifacts within your games.

Although Robux can be purchased with real money, it cannot be redeemed for cash. However, creators of games that are popular and attract a large number of players may be eligible to participate in Roblox’s “Developer Exchange” (DevEx) which allows them to convert Robux into real cash.
