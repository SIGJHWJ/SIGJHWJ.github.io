Has Delta Executor’s iOS Version Been Released?

Yes, Delta Executor’s IOS version has finally been released for iPhone and iPad users. Earlier, it was only available for Android and Windows devices.

How to Download Delta iOS Executor

Unfortunately, the process of downloading and using Delta X or Delta Executor on an IOS device is a bit different than Android.

You simply can’t download or install Delta X on IOS like Android devices. To download and run it, you need to use an application like Scarlet, Esign, Sideloadly and Apple Configuarator 2.

Installing Delta Using ESign App

  • First, visit Delta’s official site – www.delta-executor.com.
  • After that, click on the iOS button.
  • There, you need to click on the Download button to download the latest version.
  • After downloading Delta’s IPA file, download the Esign app.
  • To do so, visit its official site – www.esign-ios.com.
  • There, you need to scroll down and click on the ESign Link button.
  • To learn how to download the ESign iOS app, watch the above video.
  • Once you have successfully downloaded the app, launch the ESign app.
  • When you launch it, you might see a pop up message that reads, ‘Untrusted Enterprise Developer’.
  • If you see this message, go to Settings > General > VPN and Device Management.
  • There, you will see a couple of Untrusted Enterprise App.
  • You will have to Trust the Enterprise App.

  • Once done, launch the ESign app again.
  • This time, click on the Three Dot located at the top right corner of the screen.
  • After that, click on the Import option.
  • Now, go to Browser and Import the Delta IPA file.
  • Once imported, go to Apps section and tap on the Roblox Delta app and select Signature.
  • In a new window, tap on the Signature option again.
  • Once the process is completed, click on the Install button.
  • Doing this will begin Delta installing on your iOS device.
  • Once installed, launch it and exploit your favorite game.
  • That’s it.

Installing Delta Using Scarlet App

  • The first thing you need to do is download Delta’s IPA file by clicking on the below Download button.
Download v2.678 IPA
  • After that, visit Usescarlet.com.
  • After visiting this site, download the Scarlet app.
  • Once downloaded, install it on your iPhone.
  • Once installed, open it.
  • Now, go to Settings and then navigate o General > VPN & Device Management.
  • Now, tap on the ‘Enterprise App’ which can be found under the profile option.
  • After that, tap on the Trust option.
  • Now, you need to tap on the Sideload icon which is located at the top right corner.
  • Now, tap on the Delta.ipa file and begin the installation process.
  • Once installed, launch Delta Roblox app and enjoy.
  • That’s it.
